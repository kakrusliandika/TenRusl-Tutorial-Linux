Cookie & Local Storage Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-19

This app may use browser storage and similar web mechanisms for essential operation, preferences, and offline capability.

Storage categories

Type Purpose
Essential Files and values required for core rendering, navigation, and stability.
Preferences Settings such as theme, language, and UI state.
Offline cache Cached app assets and example files for repeat visits and offline fallback.

Manage preferences

You can clear browser storage, site data, or service-worker cache from your browser settings and site controls.
